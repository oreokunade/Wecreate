Why CONVERGE exists

Abuja is not a satellite of anywhere. It has founders building serious companies, capital that has never been properly introduced to them, and a policy community sitting closer to both than in any other city in the country.

CONVERGE puts them in the same room for one day and makes the introductions that should have happened years ago.

The four zones

Capital

Investors, funds, angels and the founders raising from them. Frank conversation about what actually gets funded in this market.

Builders

Product, engineering and the craft of building. For the people writing the code and shipping the thing.

Enterprise

Corporates, procurement and the commercial partnerships that turn a startup into a business.

Policy

Regulators, agencies and the people whose decisions become your constraints. Access, not commentary.
